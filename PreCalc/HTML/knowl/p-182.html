The symbol \(\gt\) is called an inequality symbol, and the statement \(a\gt b\) is called an inequality. There are four inequality symbols:

\begin{align*} \amp\gt \amp\amp\text{is greater than}\\ \amp\lt \amp\amp\text{is less than}\\ \amp\ge \amp\amp\text{is greater than or equal to}\\ \amp\le \amp\amp\text{is less than or equal to} \end{align*}
