  1. Let the can have radius \(r\) and height \(h\text{.}\)

  2. V\(V = \pi r^2 h\text{;}\) \(S = 2 \pi r^2 + 2 \pi r h\text{;}\) \(C = 2 \pi r^2 \cdot 0.027 + 2 \pi r h \cdot 0.015\text{.}\)

  3. \(C(r) = 0.054 \pi r^2 + 0.48 \frac{1}{r}\text{,}\) \(r \gt 0\text{.}\)

  4. \(r = \sqrt[3]{ \frac{0.48}{0.108 \pi} } \approx 1.12259\text{;}\) \(h \approx 4.041337\text{;}\) minimum cost \(C(1.12259) \approx 0.64137\text{.}\)
