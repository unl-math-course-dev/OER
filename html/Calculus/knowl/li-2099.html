Item3.3.4.c

On the interval from \(t = 0\) to \(t = 2\pi\text{,}\) \(s\) completes nearly two full periods, and thus achieves each of its maximum and minimum values. The global maximum is \(8\) as determined in (a), and the global minimum is \(2\text{.}\) These occur, for example, at \(s(\frac{5\pi}{12}) = 8\text{,}\) while the global min is \(s(\frac{11\pi}{12}) = 2\text{.}\) There are other points at which the function achieves these values on the given interval.
