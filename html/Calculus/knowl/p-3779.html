  2. \(\int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{2}} \sin(x) \, dx\)

  3. \(\int_0^1 e^x \, dx\)

  4. \(\int_{-1}^{1} x^5 \, dx\)

  5. \(\int_0^2 (3x^3 - 2x^2 - e^x) \, dx\)
