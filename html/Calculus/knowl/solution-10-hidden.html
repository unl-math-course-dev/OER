Estimating the values of the limits with tables is straightforward and should suggest the exact values stated below.

  1. \(\lim_{x \to 1} \frac{x^2 - 1}{x-1} = \lim_{x \to 1} \frac{(x+1)(x-1)}{x-1} = \lim_{x \to 1} (x+1) = 2\text{.}\)
  2. \begin{align*} \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{(2+x)^3 - 8}{x} \amp = \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{8 + 12x + 6x^2 + x^3 - 8}{x} = \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{12x + 6x^2 + x^3}{x}\\ \amp = \lim_{x \to 0} (12 + 6x + x^2) = 12\text{.} \end{align*}
  3. \begin{align*} \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{\sqrt{x+1} - 1}{x} \amp = \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{\sqrt{x+1} - 1}{x} \cdot \frac{\sqrt{x+1} + 1}{\sqrt{x+1} + 1} = \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{x+1-1}{x(\sqrt{x+1}+1)}\\ \amp = \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{1}{\sqrt{x+1}+1} = \frac{1}{2}\text{.} \end{align*}
