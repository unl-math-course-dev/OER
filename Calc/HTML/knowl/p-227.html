Using the standard formula for average velocity, we see that

\begin{align*} AV_{[1,1+h]} &= \frac{s(1+h)-s(1)}{h}\\ &= \frac{(100\cos(0.75(1+h)) \cdot e^{-0.2(1+h)}+100) - (100\cos(0.75) \cdot e^{-0.2}+100)}{h}\\ &= \frac{100\cos(0.75(1+h)) \cdot e^{-0.2(1+h)}+100 - 100\cos(0.75) \cdot e^{-0.2}-100)}{h}\\ &= \frac{100\cos(0.75(1+h)) \cdot e^{-0.2(1+h)} - 100\cos(0.75) \cdot e^{-0.2}}{h} \end{align*}
