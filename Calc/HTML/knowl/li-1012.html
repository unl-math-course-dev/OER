Item2.1.13.f

Our work suggests that \(\frac{d}{dx}[a^x] = a^x \cdot \ln(a)\text{,}\) and when \(a = e \approx 2.71828\text{,}\) it follows that

\begin{equation*} \frac{d}{dx}[e^x] = e^x \cdot \ln(e) = e^x \end{equation*}

Thus, \(e^x\) is a very special function: its derivative is itself!
