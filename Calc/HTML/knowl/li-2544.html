Item4.3.8.a

We observe that \(v(t) = 0\) at \(t = 0\text{,}\) \(t = 1\text{,}\) and \(t = 3\text{,}\) and \(v(t)\) is positive on the intervals \(0 \lt t \lt 1\) and \(3 \lt t \lt 4\text{,}\) while \(v(t)\) is negative on \(1 \lt t \lt 3\text{.}\) The total change in position, \(P\text{,}\) of the object is thus given by

\begin{equation*} P = \int_0^1 v(t) \, dt + \int_1^3 v(t) \, dt + \int_3^4 v(t) \, dt = \int_0^4 v(t) \, dt\text{.} \end{equation*}
