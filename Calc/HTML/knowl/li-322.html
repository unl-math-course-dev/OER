Item1.3.8.c

We will say that today's date is July 1, 2015, which means that \(t = 22.5\text{,}\) so we want to compute \(P'(22.5)\text{.}\) The limit definition tells us that

\begin{equation*} P'(22.5) = \lim_{h \to 0} \frac{115(1.014)^{22.5+h}-115(1.014)^{22.5}}{h}\text{.} \end{equation*}

Estimating this limit by using small values of \(h\text{,}\) we determine that \(P'(22.5) \approx 0.02186\text{,}\) which is measured in billions of people per year.
